Know the Coach

Rituu Balyann Chaudhry

A driving force for transformation, has empowered over 50,000 individuals in the past five years. With an innovative approach, Rituu introduced Mind Yoga to IPL and state-level cricketers as well as pro-Kabbadi coaches and players, reshaping their mindset towards mental fitness. Beyond sports, Rituu conducts Peak Performance Workshops in schools, colleges, and even within the Indian Defence Forces, guiding officers and their families towards holistic well-being. Through her signature programs, The Top 1% Sports Champions and The Top 1% Study Hacker, she helps sportspersons and students unlock their full potential and achieve peak performance, enabling them to reach their desired goals.
